AAAI27: AI co-pilot that generates, refines, and submits research ideas aligned with AAAI 2027 tracks.

- Mine 5 years of AAAI proceedings to extract trending topics and gaps
- Use LLM agents to produce novel, scoped project proposals with baselines
- Score ideas on feasibility, novelty, and track fit via automated review
- Auto-generate LaTeX abstracts and supplementary code skeletons
- Package as open CLI tool with export to OpenReview format
